Do you take reservations?

Yes! - We accomodate for groups small or large. Give us a call and we will do our best for you.

Do you deliver?

No. - We personally do not deliver, however, we are on the Doordash app and deliveries are made possible through it.

My meal was great/terrible. I want to tell someone about it. How do I go about doing this?

Easy! Please see our contact page and complete the form to let us know about your experience. Good or bad, we want to know!

Can I buy giftcards?

Absolutely! - Stop on by anytime and any member of our staff would be glad to help you in purchasing a giftcard. Our giftcard amounts range from $5 up to $50.
