On his life savings throughout the years and a small loan, Montoya bought a property closing down and made his decision without hesitation. He struggled initially taking on the role of owning his own business, but just months after opening El Burrito Macho he began to feel at ease. 1 year in, he finally made a profit. While expensive, this decision paid off and slowly but surely Montoya paid back his initial loan. Montoya's dedication and business-first mindset lead to rapid growth and expansion throughout Mexico and eventually to the United States.
